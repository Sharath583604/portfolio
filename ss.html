The House of Bindu's Delightful Journey

Established in 1987 by Mr. Sathya Shankar, The House of Bindu traces its roots to an automobile spare parts shop, evolving into a dynamic conglomerate. Diversifying into auto finances with Praveen Capital in 1994, Mr. Shankar ventured into manufacturing with Bindu Mineral Water, laying the foundation for a delicious legacy.
